About Us

  • We are called to the ministry of raising men from their lowest point and effecting a positive change

    The Towdah was founded on the 25th of September, 2015 by a group of 5. The Towdah was formerly known as instruments of praise.
    The Towdah is a ministry with the mantle of changing lives with the passion of praise. The Towdah holds a yearly programme themed "Wings of Worship", which is a gathering of believers focused on leading people into the atmosphere of worship and communicating with the father in prayers.
    The Towdah is divided into various arms which include:

    Worship Experience

    MasterClass and Skills Acquisition

    Events

    Music Academy
